Lasagna


Description

The traditional Riq and Carol bolognese lasagna


Ingredients:

  • Minced meat
  • cheese
  • ham
  • onions
  • garlic
  • bell pepper
  • heavy sauce
  • white sauce
  • tomato sauce
  • lasagna dough

Steps

  1. Cut a onion, half of a bell pepper and some garlic.
  2. fry the onion, the bell pepper and the garlic
  3. add the meat and a bit of salt
  4. after the meat is done, add tomato sauce and a bit of salt
  5. add the heavy sauce
  6. take a form and grease it with some of the sauce of the meat
  7. layer of lasagna dough
  8. layer of the meat
  9. layer of cheese
  10. layer of ham
  11. repeat it until the form is almost full
  12. put white sauce above the last layer of lasagna dough
  13. put the form in the oven in 180c for about 20 minutes.
  14. done.
