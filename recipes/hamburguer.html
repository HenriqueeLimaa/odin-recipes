Hamburger

Description

The traditional Riq hamburger

Ingredients:

  • Minced meat
  • cheese
  • onions
  • garlic
  • hamburger bread
  • tomato
  • lettuce
  • ketchup
  • mayo

Steps

  1. Cut a onion and some garlic.
  2. mix it with the minced meat
  3. make about 100 grams balls
  4. take a pan and put some butter to fry the breads
  5. take a pan and use some butter or oil to fry the meat balls
  6. smash the meat balls to make them look like a hamburger
  7. when the hamburger gets almost done, put cheese above it to melt
  8. make a sauce with mayo and ketchup and pass it into the bread
  9. put the hamburger into the bread
  10. put lettuce above the hamburger
  11. put tomato slices above the lettuce
  12. close the hamburger
  13. done.
